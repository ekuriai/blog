## Why Not Just Self-Host OpenClaw?

You can. OpenClaw is open source and free to run.

But self-hosting means:
- Setting up a server
- Managing updates
- Debugging when things break at 3am
- Configuring tunnels, memory, tools, and integrations

Ekuri is the managed version. We deal with all of that. You just use your agent.

It's the same reason people use Vercel instead of managing their own servers — the tool is free, the convenience is what you're paying for.
